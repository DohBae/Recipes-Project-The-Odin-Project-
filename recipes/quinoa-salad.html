Quinoa Salad

Here is my world famous quinoa salad recipe! Refreshing, light, and healthy, it's quick and easy to make, and is a perfect dish to make for a barbeque.

Ingredients:

Salad Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 1 tsp of better than boulion vegetable flavour
  • 1/2 tbsp. cumin
  • 1/2 tbsp. tumeric
  • 1 tbsp. paprika
  • 1 cucumber cubed
  • 1 tomato cubed
  • 1 bell pepper (red, yellow, or green) cubed
  • 1 red onion minced
  • Fresh cilantro (optional and to taste)
  • Fetta cheese (optional and to taste)

Dressing Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of lemon juice
  • 1 clove of minced garic
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 bunches of lemon thym minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
